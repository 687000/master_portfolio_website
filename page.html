About Me

image

I'm a graduate student pursuing my Master of Arts in Interaction & UI/UX from the Academy of Art University.

I enjoy expressing myself through drawing, reading, and coding. One of the things I love about front-end development and UI/UX design is how they allow me to combine my technical skills with my artistic side.

As a student, I am developing my skills in designing and developing intuitive and user-friendly websites using Figma, Photoshop, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Ruby on Rails. Whether I'm working on a website or an app, I'm always looking for ways to make my work both functional and aesthetically pleasing.
